## Key Marketing Copy (from audit — use in rebuild)

### The Hexagonal Advantage
> The hexagonal design isn't just aesthetic — it's a measurable engineering advantage.
> At the same 1.75" envelope width, a hexagonal cross-section delivers **+10.3% more** in every dimension that matters:
> - +10.3% more internal volume → more gas expansion → quieter shots
> - +10.3% more inner surface area → faster heat dissipation → cooler gas → quieter report
> - Six flat faces → wrench-free mounting. No tools needed.
> - Corner turbulence → disrupts laminar gas flow, further decelerating exhaust gases

| Metric (at 1.75" width, 6" length) | Round | Hexagonal | Gain |
|------------------------------------|-------|-----------|------|
| Cross-sectional area | 2.41 sq in | 2.65 sq in | +10.3% |
| Internal volume | 14.43 cu in | 15.91 cu in | +10.3% |
| Inner surface area | 32.99 sq in | 36.37 sq in | +10.3% |
